POSTERS

This is a poster series of a play named “Teen Patton Ki Kahani” It is a theatrical play that follows the downfall of three small time gamblers whose greed turns friendship into betrayal. These poster captures the play's key moments through a cinematic, symbolic and psychological lens.

This travel poster captures the enchanting charm of Udaipur, inviting travelers to explore the city where royal heritage and natural beauty come together. The mustache icon adds a touch of Rajasthani culture and pride, and the ‘Namaste’ symbol represents the warm hospitality that greets every visitor.

This poster celebrates water as a symbol of resilience, renewal and strength. The design contracts darkness and light to show both struggle and hope. It calls us to remember that, like water even we can Cry, Cleanse and Flow

Roceylon is a hypothetical premium Sri Lankan tea brand inspired by the elegance of the Rococo art movement. The muted green tones reflect freshness and purity, while the vintage portrait and ornate detailing highlight authenticity and premium quality. The poster complements the packaging by evoking a calm, artisanal tea experience, truly “Pure Ceylon Heritage in Every Cup.”
