PACKAGING

This is a Packaging design for a haircare brand named Arata. The hemp + avocado oil packaging combines green, earthy textures to reflect plant based ingredients. Illustration of diverse women highlight inclusivity showing the product is suitable for all hair types.

This Packaging design for House of Candy is both playful and appealing. The die-cut flap make the packaging visually distinctive and practical to use. The vibrant yellow base and colorful accents create an inviting and joyful appearance, suitable for a candy brand. The swirls, dots, and waves symbolize the fun and sweetness associated with candies, reinforcing the theme.
